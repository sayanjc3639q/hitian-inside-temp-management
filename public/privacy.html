Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Hitian Inside Management ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our web application to log in via Google OAuth.

1. Information We Collect

When you log in to our application using your Google Account, we receive certain profile information from Google. This information includes:

  • Your email address (used for account identification and authentication status verification).
  • Your full name (used to match and link your credentials to our internal club roster card).
  • Your profile picture URL (used as an avatar icon on your user profile page).
  • A unique identifier provided by Google (your Google User ID).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to provide, operate, and maintain our application, specifically to:

  • Identify you and grant secure access to our project dashboards.
  • Associate your authenticated account with your specific operational record card in our database.
  • Track tasks, assignments, and contributions related to club events.

We do not use your information for marketing purposes, and we do not sell, rent, or lease your personal data to third parties.

3. Data Retention and Deletion

Your profile data is stored securely in our database for as long as you maintain your account. If you wish to unlink your Google Account, delete your records, or have any questions about your data, you can request data deletion by contacting the system administrator.

4. Security

We implement industry-standard technical measures (provided via Supabase Auth services) to protect the security of your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
